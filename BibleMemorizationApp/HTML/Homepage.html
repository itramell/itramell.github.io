Solid Foundation

Tower Building Game

How to Play:

To play this game, drag the words/phrases from the word bank to the ground to write out the memory verse from beginning to end to build your tower.

The game is scored on the number of words placed correctly on the first try and ends once the tower is built. The reveal answer button shows the whole verse; however, use this as a last resort!

Each level (the default is level 2) gets progressively harder by breaking up the verse into smaller pieces and there are 5 additional modes for extra practice.

Press play to get started. Best of luck!
